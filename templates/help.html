 Introduction: all 6 faces of the cube is displayed in 2 graphs, each representing 3 faces one can observe from one corner. The top side of the cube is topmost one of the first graph (red), the bottom side is the bottommost of the second graph (green), and the remainings are the 4 cube faces on the side. Yellow side is opposite to the magenta side, and blue is opposite to cyan. Z axis runs from top (red) to bottom (green), X axis from yellow to magenta, and Y axis from blue to cyan. Left and right rotation are with respect to the directions of each axis.
